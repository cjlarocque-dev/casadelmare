Explore the Intracoastal Waterway and pristine waters with scenic paddling routes suitable for all skill levels.

Deep sea fishing and inshore fishing excursions with experienced captains in the Atlantic Ocean.

High-speed fun on the water with professionally maintained jet skis and safety equipment.

Attractions

Barefoot Landing

Entertainment complex with shopping, dining, live music venues, and fun attractions on the waterfront.

North Beach Pier & Boardwalk

Scenic oceanfront pier perfect for fishing, walking, and enjoying stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean.

Alligator Farm Zoo

Educational wildlife facility featuring alligators, exotic animals, and interactive experiences for all ages.

Nature & Wildlife

Huntington Beach State Park Trails

Scenic nature trails through pristine coastal ecosystems perfect for bird watching and wildlife observation.

Osprey & Shorebird Watching

Prime locations along the coast for spotting osprey, herons, egrets, and other migratory birds year-round.

Salt Marsh Kayaking Tours

Paddling through pristine salt marshes to observe fiddler crabs, fish, and native wetland vegetation.

North Inlet-Winyah Bay Reserve

Protected estuarine reserve offering opportunities to explore marine habitats and observe diverse wildlife.

Dolphin & Marine Life Sightings

Natural dolphin encounters throughout the intracoastal waterways and ocean, often visible from docks and piers.
